# OCL League Rules

---

## 1. Match Format
Teams consist of twelve men in the form of six two-man teams, including the club professional. Matches are played as fourballs with the nassau system:

| Segment | Points |
|---------|--------|
| Front Nine | 1 |
| Back Nine | 1 |
| 18 Hole Match | 1 |
| **Total per Match** | **3** |

| Result | Match Points Awarded |
|--------|----------------------|
| Win (>9 game points) | 1 |
| Tie (9-9 game points) | 0.5 |
| Loss | 0 |

---

## 2. Who Can Play
- Minimum age for participation is **21**
- Players must have a golfing privilege at their club
- Club membership designation is set by the first match played in the year
- A player may only represent **one club per year**
- To be eligible for playoff matches, a player must have played in **at least one regular season match**

---

## 3. Playing Tees and Order
The tees played and team order (1–6) are at the discretion of the **home captain**. If the order deviates from 1–6, the home captain must notify the away captain **at least 48 hours in advance**.

---

## 4. Handicaps and Eligibility
| Team | Format | Max Index | Min Index |
|------|--------|-----------|-----------|
| #1 | Scratch | — | — (includes club pro) |
| #2 | Scratch | — | — |
| #3 | Scratch | — | — |
| #4 | Net | 13.0 | — |
| #5 | Net | 13.0 | — |
| #6 | Net | 23.0 | 9.0 |

**Eligibility window:** Players who meet eligibility requirements **7 days before the match** may play even if a future revision changes their eligibility. Actual handicaps are based on the current revision at match time.

**Edge cases:**
- Team #6 player drops below 9.0 index → plays at their actual lower index
- Teams #4/#5 player exceeds 13.0 → plays to **13.0**
- Any net match player exceeds 23.0 → plays to **23.0**

**Stroke calculation:** Course handicaps are based on the slope rating for the tees played. Handicap strokes are calculated at **80% of the difference from the low man in the foursome**.

---

## 5. Mother Nature
The home team decides whether a match is played in inclement weather. Every effort should be made to play. If the home course is open and operating normally, the match should be played.

> If a match is not played within 1 week of Labor Day, it will be cancelled and both teams earn **0 points**.

---

## 6. Reporting Results
The **home team captain** reports results to the OCL commissioner immediately following play. The commissioner will post results via **@oclcommish** and update the website standings.

---

## 7. Playoffs
The **top 4 teams** by match points qualify for the playoffs:
- **1 vs 4** at team 1's home course
- **2 vs 3** at team 2's home course
- Finals at the higher seed's home course

**Tie-breaker order for seeding:**
1. Match points
2. Total game points
3. Total game points from away matches
4. Coin flip

**Deadlines:**
- Round 1 must be completed by **end of September**
- Finals must be completed by **end of October**

---

## 8. Playoffs — No Ties
Playoff matches require a winner. In the event of a tie, each team captain selects **two players** (excluding professionals) who played that day. They compete in **sudden death at scratch**.
