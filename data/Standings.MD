## The Standings Page lists the overall league standings in table format

** The page should order the teams by most "points"

| Team | Wins | Ties | Losses | Points | Total Match Points |

** The Rules.MD explains the points per match
** Points in this table are caluclated by the numbers of wins (2 points) and ties (1 point)
** Total Match Points is the sum of all match level points from original matches from Results
