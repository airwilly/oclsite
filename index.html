Welcome

The Old Colony Four Ball League is a competitive inter-club golf league among nine South Shore clubs. Matches are played on Saturdays and Sundays throughout the season, with teams competing for points in the four-ball format.

Use the navigation above to view the schedule and results, check current standings, or review the league rules and games.
