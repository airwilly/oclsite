Welcome

Founded in the 1950s, the Old Colony Four Ball League is Massachusetts’ South Shore inter-club fourball golf league. Member clubs are Black Rock CC, Cohasset GC, Duxbury YC, Hatherly CC, Marshfield CC, Plymouth CC, Scituate CC, Thorny Lea CC, and Wollaston GC.
